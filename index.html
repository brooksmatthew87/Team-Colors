Team Color Differences

Christen Bromberg once implied that all my favorite teams shared colors are the same. I will provide direct evidence here to show the differences between all 10 of my favorite sports teams colors.

I will admit that there are a lot of shared colors among the teams I follow, even more so than I realized. 7 out of the 10 teams have a shade of blue as a primary color, and 5 out of 10 have a shade of red. However, I cannot stand by and let Christen imply that "they are all the same color of blue" or any other such slander.

Below, I will compare each color among my teams side by side so the differences will be much clearer to the casual observer (Christen Bromberg).

First up, the Blues!

Buffalo Bills: "Blue"

Detroit Lions: "Honolulu Blue"

New York Giants: "Dark Blue"

Atlanta Braves: "Navy"

Kansas City Royals: "Royal Blue"

Los Angeles Angels: "Midnight Blue

Toronto Blue Jays: "Blue"

Toronto Blue Jays: "Navy Blue"

You will no doubt notice that the Blue Jays have two shades of blue in their color scheme, which is fitting.

Next, we have the Reds!

Buffalo Bills: "Red"

New York Giants: "Red"

Atlanta Braves: "Scarlet"

Los Angeles Angels: "Red"

Los Angeles Angels: "Maroon"

Toronto Blue Jays: "Red"

Here, the Angels are the team with double shades of one color. But I would rather draw attention to the Braves here, with their fancy use of "Scarlett"
