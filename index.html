Team Color Differences

Christen Bromberg once implied that all my favorite teams shared colors are the same. I will provide direct evidence here to show the differences between all 10 of my favorite sports teams colors.

I will admit that there are a lot of shared colors among the teams I follow, even more so than I realized. 7 out of the 10 teams have a shade of blue as a primary color, and 5 out of 10 have a shade of red. However, I cannot stand by and let Christen imply that "they are all the same color of blue" or any other such slander.

Below, I will compare each color among my teams side by side so the differences will be much clearer to the casual observer (Christen Bromberg).

First up, the Blues!

Buffalo Bills: "Blue"

Detroit Lions: "Honolulu Blue"

New York Giants: "Dark Blue"

Atlanta Braves: "Navy"

Kansas City Royals: "Royal Blue"

Los Angeles Angels: "Midnight Blue

Toronto Blue Jays: "Blue"

Toronto Blue Jays: "Navy Blue"

You will no doubt notice that the Blue Jays have two shades of blue in their color scheme, which is fitting.

Next, we have the Reds!

Buffalo Bills: "Red"

New York Giants: "Red"

Atlanta Braves: "Scarlet"

Los Angeles Angels: "Red"

Los Angeles Angels: "Maroon"

Toronto Blue Jays: "Red"

Here, the Angels are the team with double shades of one color. But I would rather draw attention to the Braves, with their fancy use of "Scarlett"

Now, the Blacks!

Am I allowed to say that?

New Orleans Saints: "Black"

Las Vegas Raiders: "Raiders Black"

Chicago White Sox: "Black"

I mean, come on, Christen! My teams don't even use the same shade of black! Look at that diversity of color!

The Silver lining!

Las Vegas Raiders: "Raiders Silver"

Chicago White Sox: "Silver"

Los Angeles Angels: "Silver"

Detroit Lions: "Silver"

New York Giants: "Gray"

I was a little surprised myself how many teams have a shade of silver that I like. Giants allowed here with "Gray" because it didn't fit anywhere else. Besides, we all know silver is just fancy gray.

Finally, we strike Gold!

New Orleans Saints: "Old Gold"

Kansas City Royals: "Gold"

Atlanta Braves: "Gold"

I am not buying that the Braves have a gold color, but that is the official name for their yellow color. Honestly, the Braves are dripping with southern fanciness. Scarlet, Navy and Gold? Stonewall Jackson will ride again.

In conclusion.

Now I would argue that no one, in good faith, could ever say my teams are all the same color. Thank you, and good night.
